Why Complex Passwords Matters

Weak passwords with fewer characters or simple patterns are easier for attackers to guess. Use a combination of:

  • Uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Numbers
  • Special characters
  • At least 12 characters

Example: Instead of "password123", use "P@ssw0rd!23$".
